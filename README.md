# Quant Finance Projects 

This repository documents my journey into **Quantitative Finance, Algorithmic Trading, and Financial Modeling**.

Over the coming months, I will be building and sharing a series of structured projects inspired by real-world quantitative models used in financial markets — ranging from statistical strategies to machine learning and risk modeling.

---

## 📊 Project Scope

The projects in this repository are inspired by industry-relevant quant models, including:

* Monte Carlo Simulation (Price Path Modeling, VaR)
* Black-Scholes Options Pricing & Greeks
* Market Timing & Signal Optimization
* Machine Learning for Market Direction Prediction
* Macro-Based Market Forecasting
* Mean Reversion & Statistical Arbitrage
* Pairs Trading (Cointegration Strategies)
* Risk Factor Attribution (Fama-French Models)
* Value at Risk (VaR, CVaR, Stress Testing)
* Portfolio Optimization (Efficient Frontier, Sharpe Ratio)

These are based on structured frameworks similar to those used in professional quant research
